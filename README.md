# Privacy Policy – Inventory Me

Inventory Me does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Storage
All data entered into the app (including photos, files, and notes) is stored locally on the user’s device only.

## Data Collection
Inventory Me does not collect any analytics, usage data, personal information, or identifiers.

## Accounts
No account or sign-in is required to use the app.

## Sharing
Photos and files are only shared when the user explicitly chooses to share them using the system share options.

## Third Parties
Inventory Me does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
